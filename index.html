One year cycling

These month I'm really excited that I'm completing my first year cycling after my adult life. I'm a software developer in a lazy life

I choosed the bike to avoid the traffic and instead of just be a tool, I get in love with cycling again.

As on my childhood it's awesome and I can do it everyday :)

I cycled at least 6500 KM this year and it was awesome. My body and mind are thankfully of that. I'm feeling happier and thinking more about how many cool stuff I'll do cycling.

The question is: bike is freedom.

When you start riding and observe how many blockers you skip by byke, you start realizing you can use it more and more. It's more simple, cheaper and healthly.

I crossed Floripa island multiple times and now I think I know more about the city than a good part of people that only commute by cars or bus.

When you walk, run or ride, you get a slow point of view and can appreciate more accuratedly each pieace of land you pass. By cars or bus you just use the way. You're not really there, being part of the scene.
